Do cats know their name?

Yes! According to multiple veterinarians and studies, cats do indeed recognize and know their own name.


Essential tips to raise healthy cats

Do not overfeed them, pet them a lot, take good care of them, and give them lots of love.


Why does my cat attack me?

Cats can attack for a variety of reasons, but usually cats are just very playful and biting is one of the ways they play with you.


How to throw a birthday party for your cat

Treat your cat very well, but don't bake them a cake, as choclate is not very good for cats.
